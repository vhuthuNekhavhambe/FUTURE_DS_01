# 📊 Business Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard

A Power BI dashboard developed as part of the **Future Interns Data Science & Analytics Internship – Task 1**. This project focuses on analyzing business sales data to uncover actionable insights that can support data-driven decision-making.

## 📌 Project Overview

The objective of this project was to transform raw sales data into an interactive dashboard that highlights key business performance metrics. Using Power BI, I cleaned and analyzed the data to identify revenue trends, top-performing products, profitable regions, and customer purchasing patterns.

## 🎯 Objectives

- Clean and prepare raw sales data
- Analyze sales performance over time
- Identify top-selling products
- Evaluate regional sales and profitability
- Build an interactive dashboard for business insights
- Present findings through data storytelling

## 📈 Dashboard Features

The dashboard includes:

- **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)**
  - Total Sales
  - Total Quantity Sold
  - Total Profit
  - Profit Margin (%)

- **Sales Analysis**
  - Sales by Region
  - Sales Trend by Year and Quarter
  - Top Products by Sales
  - Top Customers by Sales

- **Profit Analysis**
  - Profit by Region

- **Interactive Filters**
  - Customer Segment
  - Product Category
  - Ship Mode

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- **Power BI**
- Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation)
- DAX (Measures and Calculations)

## 📊 Key Insights

- The **East** and **West** regions generated the highest sales and profit.
- Technology products contributed significantly to total revenue.
- Sales showed an upward trend over the analyzed period.
- A small group of customers accounted for a large share of total sales, highlighting high-value customers.

## Recommendations

- The business should continue investing in the East and West regions through targeted marketing campaigns and customer loyalty programs.
- Consider localised marketing campaigns or special offers to boost sales in the south region since it recorded the lowest sales and profit.
- The business should implement customer loyalty programs,personalized promotions and exclusive discounts to encourage repeat purchases. 
- The business should ensure that products that contribute a significant portion of total sales should always be stocked and feature them prominently in marketing campaigns.
